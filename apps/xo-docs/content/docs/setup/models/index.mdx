The **Models** tab is where you connect a model provider to your XO project. Two paths:

- **OAuth** — sign in with your Claude or ChatGPT subscription. Best if you already pay for one of those plans.
- **API Key** — paste a key from Anthropic, OpenAI, or OpenRouter. Best for pay-as-you-go access or for providers without an OAuth flow.

Credentials live in your project's workspace, not on the XO platform.
