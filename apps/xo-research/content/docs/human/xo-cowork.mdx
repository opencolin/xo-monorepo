Experimental · Powered by OpenClaw · No coding required

XO Cowork is the fastest way to a working AI coworker. Launch it from the platform and you have a real workspace running within 10 minutes, with no wiring required.

## What you get

- **Command it from anywhere**: WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, or the XO Cowork UI.
- **Switch models freely**: Claude, OpenAI, and 100+ providers.
- **Work from your IDE**: SSH seamlessly into the workspace from VS Code and other editors.

## Why it's built this way

**A real harness, not a chat wrapper.** The template hands you `xo-cowork-api` (the harness that bridges context windows, holds files, and brokers the runtime), the open-source `xo-coworker` UI, and OpenClaw as the runtime. The runtime never runs bare; the harness mediates it.

**The harness, not the model, sets how long an agent usefully runs.** That is the core finding of our [agent harness research](/agent-harness). XO Cowork exists so you get that harness for free instead of building it yourself.

**Pluggable runtime.** OpenClaw, Claude Code, and Hermes all plug into the same harness, so you are not locked to one model or provider.

**Open source, no black box.** `xo-coworker` is MIT licensed and self-hostable. The template is just the fast path to something you could stand up yourself, and inspect end to end.

## Launch it

Projects → Create → **XO Cowork** → **Create Agent**. You will have a coworker running within 10 minutes.
