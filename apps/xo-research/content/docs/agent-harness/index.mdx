## Why this area exists

XO's product is the harness. Two paired layers:

- `xo-coworker` plus `xo-cowork-api` is the workspace: the substrate that bridges context windows, holds files, brokers runtimes.
- `xo-swarm` plus `xo-swarm-api` is the platform: launches workspaces, manages identity, billing, integrations.

Every choice in those layers is a harness-design choice. The research collected here is the evidence base for those choices.
